# Mountain Trail Coffee - Storyboard

## Project Info

- **Total Duration**: 12 seconds
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Visual Style**: Cinematic, warm natural lighting, photorealistic
- **Color Palette**: Warm earth tones -- amber, deep green, golden highlights
- **Audio Direction**: Soft ambient nature sounds, gentle acoustic guitar
- **Target Model**: seedance-2.0

---

## Shot 1 (0:00 - 0:04)

- **Camera**: Extreme wide shot, slow crane down through morning mist
- **Scene**: A mountain valley at sunrise. Pine trees line a winding trail. Mist rolls through the valley. Golden light breaks over the eastern ridge.
- **Action**: The camera descends through wisps of fog, revealing the landscape below. A small figure is visible on the trail in the distance.
- **Sound**: Birdsong, gentle wind through pines
- **Image Prompt**: Extreme wide shot of a mountain valley at sunrise, golden light breaking over a ridge to the east. Pine forests line both sides of a winding dirt trail. Morning mist rolls through the valley floor. A tiny lone hiker figure visible on the trail in the lower third. Photorealistic, cinematic color grading with warm amber and deep green tones. Shot from elevated crane position looking slightly downward. Soft volumetric light rays through the mist.

---

## Shot 2 (0:04 - 0:08)

- **Camera**: Medium close-up, static with shallow depth of field
- **Scene**: Hands cupping a steaming enamel mug of coffee. The mug rests on a weathered wooden camp table. Blurred pine trees and golden morning light in the background.
- **Action**: Steam rises from the dark coffee in slow, curling wisps. The hands gently lift the mug.
- **Sound**: Soft pour sound, steam hiss
- **Image Prompt**: Medium close-up of two hands cupping a dark blue enamel camping mug filled with black coffee, wisps of steam rising. The mug sits on a weathered wooden camp table. Shallow depth of field with blurred pine trees and warm golden sunrise light in the background. Photorealistic, warm color temperature. Hands are weathered, wearing a dark green flannel sleeve cuff. Soft natural side lighting from the left.

---

## Shot 3 (0:08 - 0:12)

- **Camera**: Medium shot, slow dolly out to wide shot
- **Scene**: The hiker sits on a flat rock beside the trail, coffee mug in hand, looking out over the valley. Backpack on the ground nearby. The sun is fully up, bathing the scene in warm light.
- **Action**: The hiker takes a slow sip, lowers the mug, and exhales contentedly. The camera pulls back to reveal the vast landscape stretching behind them.
- **Sound**: Acoustic guitar begins softly, ambient nature continues
- **Image Prompt**: Medium shot of a hiker sitting on a flat gray rock beside a mountain trail, holding a dark blue enamel mug in their right hand. They wear a dark green flannel shirt, khaki hiking pants, and brown boots. A worn brown backpack leans against the rock. Warm golden morning sunlight bathes the scene from the right. Behind the hiker, a vast mountain valley stretches into the distance with layered ridges fading into atmospheric haze. Photorealistic, cinematic composition with the hiker in the left third of frame. Warm amber and green color palette.

---

## Notes

- Character consistency: hiker wears the same dark green flannel and khaki pants across shots 1 and 3
- The dark blue enamel mug appears in shots 2 and 3 -- maintain visual consistency
- Shot 1 is an establishing shot; the figure is small and does not need detailed features
- For Seedance 2.0: use omni_reference mode with all 3 keyframes as image_files to maximize visual consistency
